Bootcamp 2020: Boxing Fight

My first programming project, concluding the 2 week Programming Bootcamp at City (2020). This Processing project demonstrates a comprehensive understanding of the use of images, loops, and audio . my project is compiled of a series of images which when played create a funny animation as well as includes sound effects. I used a series of if loops as well as created my own methods one for images and the other for sound. When running the sketch the images apear in order with a time frame in between. The colourful animation shows two fighters having a boxing match.
